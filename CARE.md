# Sensor Burn‑In, Storage, and Drift Management

## Burn‑In
Power sensors for 24–48 hours before first use in a well‑ventilated area.

## Pre‑Session Warm‑Up
Power on in clean air for 5–10 minutes.

## During Use
Do not touch the sensor mesh. Keep away from liquid water and silicone compounds.

## Between Samples
2‑minute air flush.

## After Use
Power down. Store entire device in an airtight container with silica gel packets or a tablespoon of dry rice wrapped in tissue.

## Long‑Term Storage (>1 month)
Re‑age: power on 48–168 hours in clean air.

## Cleaning
Never wipe the sensor mesh. If contaminated, power‑cycle and re‑age.
